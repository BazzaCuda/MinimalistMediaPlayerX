


Control | Action
------- | ------
| **[BACKSPACE]**             | reset all adjustments to their defaults:<br />audio sync, brightness, contrast, gamma, pan, rotation, saturation, speed, zoom |
| &nbsp; ||
| **[1]**                     | reset Speed to [**1**]00% |
| **[2]**  &nbsp;&nbsp;**C**  | reset **C**ontrast to normal
| **[3]**  &nbsp;&nbsp;**P**  | reset **P**an to normal |
| **[4]**  &nbsp;&nbsp;**R**  | reset **R**otation to normal/not rotated
| **[0]**                     | reset Brightness to normal
| **[']**                     | reset Gamma to normal |
| **[;]**                     | reset Saturation to normal
| **[U]**                     | [**U**]nzoom, i.e. re-fit the video to the window
