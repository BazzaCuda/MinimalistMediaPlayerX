


Control | Action
------- | ------
| **[BACKSPACE]**             | reset all adjustments to their defaults:<br />brightness, contrast, gamma, pan, rotation, saturation, speed, zoom |
| &nbsp; ||
| **[1]**                     | reset Speed to [1]00% |
| **[2]**                     | reset Contrast to normal
| **[3]**                     | reset Pan to normal |
| **[4]**                     | reset Rotation to normal/not rotated
| **[0]**                     | reset Brightness to normal
| **[']**                     | reset Gamma to normal |
| **[;]**                     | reset Saturation to normal
| **[U]**                     | [U]nzoom, i.e. re-fit the video to the window
