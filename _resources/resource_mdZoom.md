[Help Page: Adjust Image](Adjust_Image)<br />

You can zoom in and out on an image or video

- the default is to zoom in and out 1/100th of the size of the image or video frame
- if you also hold down a [Shift] key, the zoom amount will increase to 1/10th
- the zoom in/out keys can be held down for rapid repeat
- for videos, you can zoom in and out during playback and on [Freeze Frame](Freeze_Frame) (right/left arrow keys)

| Control | Action |
| ------- | ------ |
| **[BACKSPACE]**  | reset all adjustments to their defaults:<br />brightness, contrast, gamma, pan, rotation, saturation, speed, zoom |
| &nbsp; ||
| **[I]**          | zoom [I]n. Can be held down for rapid zooming in
| **[O]**          | zoom [O]ut. Can be held down for rapid zooming out
| **[U]**          | [U]nzoom, i.e. re-fit the video to the window
| add **[Shift]**  | increase zoom amount |
| &nbsp; ||
| Image & Thumbnail Browser ||
| Ctrl and **Mouse Wheel** up/down | zoom image in/out |
| add **[Shift]** | increase zoom amount |
