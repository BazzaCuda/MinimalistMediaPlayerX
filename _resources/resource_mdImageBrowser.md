Launching the **Image & Thumbnail Browser** from the **Main Media Window**

| Control | Action |
| ------- | ------ |
| &nbsp; ||
| Main Media Window ||
| Ctrl-**[I]**                | open this [I]mage in the [I]mage & Thumbnail Browser |
| **[T]**                     | on an image, open the Image & [T]humbnail Browser in Thumbnail Mode |
| **[T]**                     | on an audio or video file, [T]ab as normal |
| **[Y]**                     | any media file, open the Image & Thumbnail Browser in Thumbnail Mode |
| &nbsp; ||
| Additional ||
| Ctrl-**[F]**                | open your [F]ile Explorer at the current [F]older |
