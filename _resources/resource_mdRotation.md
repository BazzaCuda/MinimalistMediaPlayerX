Help Page: [Adjust Image](Adjust_Image)<br />

You can rotate any image or video by 45 degrees at a time

- you can rotate right/clockwise a maximum of 7 times
- that's 315 degrees, one step short of a full 360
- to rotate left/anti-clockwise, you must have rotated right/clockwise at least once

| Control | Action |
| ------- | ------ |
| **[BACKSPACE]**  | reset all adjustments to their defaults:<br />audio sync, brightness, contrast, gamma, pan, rotation, saturation, speed, zoom |
| &nbsp; ||
| **[Pg Dn]**      | rotate image or video right/clockwise (you must do this at least once before you can rotate left)
| **[Pg Up]**      | rotate image or video left/anti-clockwise (you must have first rotated right at least once)
| **[4]**          | reset rotation to normal/not rotated
